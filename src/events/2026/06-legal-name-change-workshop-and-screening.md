Did you know that changing your legal name and gender marker is free in Victoria?
Sabby will be hosting a legal name change workshop. We will provide a friendly, welcoming space where we can help you navigate the whole process:

Checking whether you are eligible for a legal name / gender marker change in Victoria,
Gathering the necessary proof of identity documents,
Filling out forms and getting supporting statements,
Making a list of places to update with your new details,
Helping interstate folks navigate the different requirements and costs for each state,

After the workshop we'll take a break for dinner and hold a screening of "Paris is Burning"
