Join Speech Pathologist Allie (any pronouns) in an interactive workshop to support your journey in discovering a voice that helps you feel confident and celebrated.

## What to expect:
The workshop will include Allie explaining her way to understand voices and voice training to support you in exploring voice as part of your identity. Throughout the workshop, there will be optional, low pressure exercises to support your understanding and help you experiment with your voice. At the end of the workshop, Allie will be able to answer any questions and provide direction as to how you can continue your gender affirming voice training journey.

## About Allie:
Allie is a trained speech pathologist (or a professional yapper as she calls it!). She has 2 years of experience of working with individuals of all ages and genders, and is an active member in organisations working in the gender affirming voice training space such as GIIV (Gender Identity In Voice) and La Trobe University. They are passionate about helping people express themselves and discover a voice that helps them feel celebrated.
