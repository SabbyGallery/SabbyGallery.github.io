Sabby Co-op Recruitment Seminar

12 Jul 2025 — from 2PM

Learn what Sabby becoming a co-op means & how to get involved!

Do you want to be best equipped to talk to your friends and others about joining the Co-op?

In this workshop you'll be given the tools and talking points to help you do just that!

What's a Co-op? Why Should you join? We'll discuss how Sabby's structure will change and how to get involved!

Where is Sabby?

When we re-open Sabby will be located at:
60 Leicester St, Carlton.

You can get to Sabby via:

  • Bike: The 60L Green Building has internal bike racks and end of trip facilities
  • Tram: 19, 57 & 59, Stop 7 - Queen Victoria Market (5 mins walk)
  • Train: Melbourne Central or State Library Stations (11 min walk or tram)
  • Bus: 200 & 207, Queensberry St & Lygon St Stop (9 mins walk) / 605, A'Beckett St & Queen St Stop (12 mins walk)
  • Car: There is limited parking outside on Leicester street. For recommendations for nearby parking in Carlton see here.
