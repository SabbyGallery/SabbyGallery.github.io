Sabby Stream Week: Hot Seat Game Jam

07 Aug 2025 — 2PM to 10PM

One Game, Too Many Cooks!

Hot Seat Game Jam! Participants will all be working collaboratively on a single game.

Roll a d20. That's how many minutes you have in the hot seat as we create one game with too many cooks. We need your skills whether you code, draw, make sounds or are a real ideas person.

Programming experience not needed, feel free to contribute by adding art, sounds, music or anything else!

Schedule

2pm Welcome To Day 4

2:30pm Hot Seat Game Jam

Rules, Brief, Roll for initative.

6pm Game-A-Week Wizard F*ckers Punks with Hugh

Hugh has made many a game during Game-A-Week, so let's find out more about Game-A-Week and one of Hugh's Games to come out of that - Wizard Punks F*ckers which also showcased at Parallels and is coming soon to Steam.

7pm WIP Night Chat w/ Max and Maize

WIP Nights are a friendly and kind space to share projects, ask questions, and to get feedback and help. Join Max and Maize for a retrospective of WIP nights and hear how they are an important part of peer support at Sabby.

Where is Sabby?

Sabby is located at:
141 Victoria Parade, Collingwood.

You can get to Sabby via:

  • Tram: 12 and 109, Stop 16 - Wellington St (2 mins walk)
  • Train: Hurstbridge and Mernda lines, North Richmond Station (7 min walk)
  • Bus: 302 - 309, 318, 905 - 908, Cromwell St Stop (2 mins walk) / 246, Victoria Pde Stop (5 mins walk)
  • Car: Limited parking is available on Victoria Parade. Two parking spots are available behind Sabby in the alley off Rokeby Street. More parking spots are available a short walk away on Gertrude Street.

(There is a 141 Victoria Parade in Fitzroy, but that is NOT Sabby! That's someone's house! Don't get it mixed up!)
