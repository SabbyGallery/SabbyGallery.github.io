TANGENTS

04 Jun 2024 — from 11AM

Serenade presents... a selection of games from NSW!

Our good friends from Sydney's Serenade are collaborating with Sabby to present their Freeplay side-show, TANGENTS!

TANGENTS is an exhibit of weird and wonderful games from NSW, fostering connections on the fringes during Freeplay week in Melbourne.

The Serenade team will be talking at Freeplay on Day One. That's Thursday 6th June at 1.30PM. Their talk will be followed by our very own Null and Siobhan who will be delivering a talk about Sabby.

Don't miss that double-header, and don't miss TANGENTS!

Where is Sabby?

Sabby is located at:
141 Victoria Parade, Collingwood.

You can get to Sabby via:

  • Tram: 12 and 109, Stop 16 - Wellington St (2 mins walk)
  • Train: Hurstbridge and Mernda lines, North Richmond Station (7 min walk)
  • Bus: 302 - 309, 318, 905 - 908, Cromwell St Stop (2 mins walk) / 246, Victoria Pde Stop (5 mins walk)
  • Car: Limited parking is available on Victoria Parade. Two parking spots are available behind Sabby in the alley off Rokeby Street. More parking spots are available a short walk away on Gertrude Street.

(There is a 141 Victoria Parade in Fitzroy, but that is NOT Sabby! That's someone's house! Don't get it mixed up!)
