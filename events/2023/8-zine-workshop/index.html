Zine Workshop

27 Aug 2023 — 2PM to 6PM

A chill and cosy afternoon of zine making!

Come along for a chill and cosy afternoon of zine making at Sabbatical Gallery! Seasoned zine maker Tegan (https://teganewebb.neocities.org/) will be around to show you some techniques and share zines from her collection for inspiration. It's gonna be fun!

Materials will be provided, but feel free to BYO any art/craft supplies you'd like to use. There might even be a typewriter (if Richie can get it working, fingers crossed but no promises!).

Where is Sabby?

Sabby is located at:
60 Leicester St, Carlton.

  • 19, 57 & 59, Stop 7 - Queen Victoria Market (5 mins walk)
  • 1, 3, 5, 6, 16, 64, 67 & 72, Stop 4 - Queensberry St (5 mins walk)
Train:
  • State Library Station (8 min walk)
  • Melbourne Central Station (11 min walk or 7 min tram)
  • Parkville Station (11 min walk or 8 min tram)
Bus:
  • 200 & 207, Queensberry St & Lygon St Stop (9 mins walk)
  • 605, A'Beckett St & Queen St Stop (12 mins walk)
Car:
There is limited parking outside on Leicester street and more on the surrounding Carlton Streets, we recommend walking, riding or taking public transit instead.
